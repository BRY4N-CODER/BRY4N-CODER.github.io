The Siren Night

By: Advent Tide Studios

I sail to an island into the unknown. For who knows what truths may behold!

The winds thrust and trow as I begin to row.

The night's gleam of stars in the sky as they be my guide

to a place of unknown danger and seed.

I wait as the wind blows me closer drinking all my mead.

Curiosity arouses me at once with many desires and wants.

I hear the most unusual sound in the night that drains all my power and might.

Tired and weary I feel as it seems like fate's seal.

A beauty on the shore as lust takes hold of me once more.

Could this be the island core?

The siren on the shore I begin to yearn for.

The majestic, seductive sound of her voice leaves me with no choice.

On the shore I lay with little hope of life I take out my knife.

My hands feel weary and feet sore as I lift my body from the island's shore.

The siren grasps me and holds me tight

as I begin to lose my sight with no sense of right and draining might.

Just then as if heaven's gift, my sword begins to lift!

The sword glows as the winter's snow as a ghost takes it and woes!

The ghost with great fury and rage sticks the knife in the siren's rib cage,

and the heart revealed! I shall deal the death blow and the final seal!

I strike with what strength restored as the siren screamed and roared!

The siren in agony screamed a final scream,

and I awake to find it was all just a dream.

I lay in my bed awake and in awe of the dream

which I shall title "The Siren Night" as I held a claw!

Was this dream something I actually lived and saw?
